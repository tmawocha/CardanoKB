About

This project builds the first structured RDF knowledge graph for Cardano's CIP-1694 on-chain governance ecosystem (Conway era). Governance data is publicly available on-chain but fragmented across many API endpoints. This knowledge graph unifies it into a queryable structure that supports semantic reasoning about governance health, voting power distribution, and actor relationships.

Key Findings

  • ~26.8% of active stake is delegated to AlwaysAbstain (5.71 billion ADA), likely driven by custodial platforms delegating on behalf of users.
  • 54.5% of registered DReps have never cast a single vote, revealing a significant participation gap.
  • Multiple proposals were rejected with strong No majorities, demonstrating substantive governance scrutiny.
  • Whale delegation is measurable — one DRep accumulated 292 million ADA from just 90 delegators.
